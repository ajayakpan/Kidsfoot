Radio Nigeria Kids Football Challenge

As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), Radio Nigeria South South Zone under the leadership of Engr. Dr. Jonas Emechebe decided to introduce the Kids Football Challenge to help discover young talent and considering how important sports is to Nigeria, and in line with our motor of Uplifting the People and Uniting the Nation, it only seemed like a natural choice to use football at this level.

The Challenge is organised by the LOC led by Kola Oredipe , veteran journalist as the Chairman LOC.
Kelechi Chuks-Osuji , a seasoned Marketing Executive is the Vice Chairman while Abiodun Omiyale functions as the Secretary of the LOC

Past Winners

The inuagural winner of the Radio Nigeria Kids Football Challenge is Great Calvary International School, Rumueme, Port Harcourt

The Challenge

Group Stage

All 24 teams will be grouped in 6 groups of 4 teams each and faceoff each team member

Knockout Stage

The top 2 teams will qualify automatically for the round of 16, to be joined by the best 4 third place finishers

Winners of each round of 16 fixture will play until they get to the final

The Finals

The winner in the finals will go home with prize money and a giant trophy. the final match will be preceeded by the third place playoffs.
